About Us

Home Brite is Bowling Green's most trusted pressure washing and restoration service. We are a customer-first company and you are always a priority. Our team is professional, trained, and ready to take care of all your needs. We are experts in cleaning home exteriors, patios, decks, driveways, and more. With our custom softwash process, we take care of your vinyl and softer surface and we only use hot water with concrete to give you the best clean.
